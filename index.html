    Solubility?Group?

    1. Solubility of the salt and starting materials
      • All Group 1 and ammonium salts are soluble.
      • All nitrates are soluble.
      • All chlorides are soluble except silver and lead(II) chlorides.
      • All sulfates are soluble except calcium, lead(II) and barium sulfates.
      • All carbonates are insoluble except Group 1 and ammonium carbonates.
    2. Reactivity of the metal
      • Group 1 metals and some Group 2 metals react explosively/violently with acids, making the reaction unsafe.
      • Cu, Ag and Au are unreactive metals, they do not react with acids.
